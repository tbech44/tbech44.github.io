Heinz Field is the home of both the Pittsburgh Steelers and the Pitt Panthers football teams since 2001. It has a capacity of 68,400 with 129 executive suites. In 2011 the stadium hosted the NHL Winter Classic. It also occasionally hosts concerts and other events. It was also the site of Pitt's recent victory over PSU.














PNC Park opened in 2001, making it one of the newer stadiums in the MLB. It cost $216 million to construct and has a capacity of 38,362. It is the home of the Pittsburgh Pirates but it also occasionally hosts concerts and has appeared in several Hollywood films.
