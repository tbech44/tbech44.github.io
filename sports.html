Heinz Field is the home of both the Pittsburgh Steelers and the Pitt Panthers football teams since 2001. It has a capacity of 68,400 with 129 executive suites. In 2011 the stadium hosted the NHL Winter Classic. It also occasionally hosts concerts and other events.

It was also the site of Pitt's recent victory over PSU.
